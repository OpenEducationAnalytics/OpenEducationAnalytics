  • OEA Partner Benefits

  • We have a community of education systems and universities around the world seeking to implement OEA and they need implementation partners!

  • As an OEA Partner, you get the following benefits:

    • OEA Technical Training and Microsoft Teams collaboration channel.
    • Quarterly OEA Partner meetings and trainings.
    • OEA technical support services.
    • Inclusion on OEA recognized partner page.
    • Access to advanced product roadmap.

  • Become an OEA Partner

  • If your organization is already a Microsoft Partner, you can sign up to join the OEA Partner Program. After filling the form, our team will get in touch with next steps. In the meantime, your data team can start learning the technologies and skills needed for OEA under the Skills and Training tab.
